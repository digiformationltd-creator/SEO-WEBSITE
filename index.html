Technical SEO FAQ

What are Core Web Vitals?
They are a set of specific factors that Google considers important in a webpage's overall user experience, including LCP (loading), FID (interactivity), and CLS (visual stability).
How long does an audit take?
A full enterprise technical audit typically takes 2-3 weeks to complete, depending on the size of your domain.
Can you work with our dev team?
Absolutely. We often provide Jira tickets and collaborate directly with in-house or external development teams.
Why is my site not indexing?
Common reasons include blocked robots.txt, excessive duplicate content, low-quality signals, or server timeout errors.
Do you support headless CMS?
Yes, we specialize in technical SEO for modern JS frameworks, including Next.js, React, and Vue applications.

Local SEO FAQ

What is the "Map Pack"?
The Map Pack (or Local Pack) is the prominent block of 3 local business results accompanied by a Google Map, displayed clearly at the top of local searches.
Do I need a physical address?
Ideally yes, but service-area businesses (like plumbers who visit clients) can also rank highly by defining a targeted service area.
How important are reviews?
Crucial. Reviews act as the primary conversion lever once you are visible, and Google considers review velocity and rating in its local algorithm.
Can you rank me in multiple cities?
Yes. Using location-specific landing pages and targeted link building, we can help you dominate multiple UK jurisdictions.
What is NAP consistency?
Name, Address, Phone must be identical across the entire web to prove legitimacy to Google's algorithm.

E-Commerce SEO FAQ

What platforms do you work with?
We primarily work with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento 2, and BigCommerce. We also handle custom headless builds.
How do you handle out of stock products?
We implement strategic soft 404s, 301 redirects, or "related products" overlays to preserve link equity and user experience.
Why is my site speed so slow?
E-commerce stores often suffer from bloated third-party apps, unoptimized heavy imagery, and render-blocking scripts. We fix all of this.
Do you write product descriptions?

What is faceted navigation?
It's the filtering system (size, color, brand) on your store. If unmanaged, it creates millions of duplicate URLs that destroy your SEO.

The Challenge

Our client, a high-growth UK fintech SaaS, had hit a plateau. Despite a strong product, they were completely invisible for their high-intent commercial keywords. Their content was informational but lacked conversion value, and their domain authority was drastically lower than legacy competitors.

The Strategy

We deployed a three-pronged attack focused purely on bottom-of-funnel (BOFU) intent.

  • Content Siloing: We restructured their /solutions/ pages into rigid hierarchical silos, strictly passing PageRank up to heavily optimized money pages.
  • Programmatic Competitor Pages: We created high-converting 'Client vs Competitor' comparison pages to siphon traffic from people actively looking to switch tools.
  • Digital PR: We executed data-led PR campaigns using their own internal fintech data, securing massive links from the FT, The Guardian, and TechCrunch.

The Results

Within 6 months, they captured the #1 spot for 15 core commercial terms. By month 12, organic search had overtaken paid channels as their highest contributor to pipeline, driving £6M in new ARR.
